#Hifocus — Subscription Packages

> Timer SaaS · 4 tiers · themes, analytics & team tools

---

## Overview

The market splits cleanly into three user types: casual users who want a simple, distraction-free timer; professionals and freelancers who need task integration and analytics; and teams that require collaboration tools and centralized tracking. FocusFlow's four tiers reflect this segmentation directly.

## Plan Details

### 🌱 Seedling — Free

A genuinely useful free tier. Not crippled — the core Pomodoro experience is clean and complete. Converts curious users into paying ones.

**Included:**
- Standard 25/5 Pomodoro timer (fixed intervals)
- Basic task list (up to 5 tasks)
- 3 default themes (light mode only)
- Browser notifications
- 7-day session history

**Not included:** custom intervals, ambient sounds, analytics dashboard, website blocker, integrations

---

### 🔵 Flow — $6/month

The conversion engine. Unlocks the features that keep users coming back daily — customizable intervals, dark mode, sounds, and real analytics.

**Includes everything in Seedling, plus:**
- Custom work/break intervals
- Unlimited tasks & projects
- 10 premium themes + dark mode
- 20+ ambient soundscapes (lofi, jazz, nature, binaural beats)
- Productivity analytics (30-day history)
- Website blocker
- Cross-device sync (up to 3 devices)
- Daily focus goals & streaks
- Auto-start sessions & breaks
- Focus mode (full screen)
- Font selector & accent colour picker

---

### 🟣 Deep Work — $14/month

Targets power users who want the deepest focus environment and productivity data. AI insights, calendar sync, and full customisation.

**Includes everything in Flow, plus:**
- Unlimited theme customisation (custom hex, background images, layout toggle)
- Full analytics history (unlimited)
- AI focus coach (daily insights & recommendations)
- Gamification — XP points & badges
- Calendar integrations (Google Calendar, Outlook)
- Slack status sync (auto-set "Do Not Disturb" during sessions)
- Exportable reports (CSV & PDF)
- Unlimited devices
- Custom sound upload
- Floating timer widget
- Screensaver mode
- Subtasks, priorities & recurring tasks
- Task templates

---

### 🟠 Studio — $10/user/month

Built for teams that need shared accountability, manager visibility, and enterprise-grade controls.

**Includes everything in Deep Work, plus:**
- Shared team workspace
- Group focus sessions (co-timer — everyone works in sync)
- Team leaderboard & shared streaks
- Manager analytics dashboard
- SSO + SAML

- Custom branding (logo, brand colours, custom domain — white-label ready)

- CSS overrides

- Billing management for the team

## Key Tier Design Principles

1. **Gate analytics depth, not analytics entirely.** Give everyone basic session counts. Reserve trends, exports, and AI insights for paid tiers — users get a taste of value before they pay.

2. **Make themes a strong upgrade hook.** Users stare at the timer for hours daily. Environment customisation drives perceived value far more than feature lists.

3. **Keep the free tier ad-free.** Monetise through features, not attention. Ads on a focus app are a trust killer.

4. **Price the team tier per-seat.** It scales naturally with company size and aligns incentives — you grow when they grow.

5. **The 14-day trial applies to Flow and Deep Work.** Let users experience the full environment before committing. Studio gets a sales-assisted trial instead.
